Tally Prime is an easy-to-use software for managing accounting, inventory, and tax compliance efficiently.

  • Easily create invoices, manage accounts, and track cash flow.
  • Allow multiple users to work simultaneously with different roles and permissions, ensuring smooth collaboration.
  • Instantly view stock levels and track stock movement, helping you avoid overstocking or stockouts.
  • An intuitive design that makes it easy to navigate and perform tasks without complex training.
  • Safeguard your financial data with regular backups, and restore it quickly in case of any issues.
  • Generate various financial reports such as balance sheets, profit & loss statements, and cash flow reports to keep track of business performance.
  • Automatically calculate taxes like VAT and TDS, and easily generate returns.
  • Ensure data protection with password-based security and restricted user access to sensitive financial information.

Busy is ideal for businesses looking for smart accounting and inventory solutions.

  • Match your books with bank statements to ensure accurate financial records.
  • Generate detailed financial reports and customize them to fit your business needs.

  • Secure your data with password protection and user access controls to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Get timely updates and support from the Busy team to ensure your software runs smoothly.
  • Integrate Busy with other software and tools to streamline your business operations.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tally Prime used for?

Tally Prime is an accounting software that helps businesses manage accounts, inventory, VAT, and payroll efficiently.

Can Tally Prime handle VAT instead of GST?

Yes, Tally Prime is customizable to handle VAT, which is used in your country.

Can Tally Prime calculate Nepali TDS?

Yes, Tally Prime can be customized to calculate and manage Nepali TDS as per your local regulations.

Does Tally Prime support remote access?

Yes, Tally Prime offers remote access, allowing you to manage your accounts from anywhere.

Is it easy to migrate data from older Tally versions to Tally Prime?

Yes, data migration from older versions like Tally ERP 9 to Tally Prime is quick and seamless.

What is Busy Software designed for?

Busy Software is used for accounting, VAT compliance, inventory management, and financial reporting.

Does Busy Software support VAT instead of GST?

Yes, Busy Software can be configured to handle VAT compliance effectively.

Can Busy Software calculate Nepali TDS?

Yes, Busy Software can be customized to manage Nepali TDS calculations and deductions.

Is Busy Software suitable for small businesses?

Yes, it is ideal for small to medium-sized businesses like wholesalers, retailers, and manufacturers.

Does Busy Software have a mobile app?

Yes, Busy Software offers a mobile app for easy access to your data on the go.
